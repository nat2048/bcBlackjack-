Dice Blackjack Rules

Tokens

  • Each player starts with 50 tokens
  • Tokens have to be displayed on easly visible sign
  • Players need to keep count of their tokens throughout the game
  • Tokens can be exchanged between players
  • Tokens are lost upon exiting the casino, relog/crash doesn't count

Game

  • Dice Blackjack combines traditional blackjack rules with dice rolls.
  • The goal is to roll higher than dealer without rolling higher than 21
  • Everybody can be a dealer but owner and staff(WIP) have priority
  • Stand on red, grab a sign and write 50 on it to become player
  • 1.Dealer takes the bets. Minimum bet is 20 tokens. If you have no money for minimal bet casino can give you a loan(if you win you keep the won amount)
  • 2.Dealer rolls one dice and players roll 2 dices.
  • Roll values: 1 = ace, value 1 or 11 benefitial to player | 2-9 value equal to number | 10 = king/queen/jack, value 10
  • 3.Player can decide to(unable to speak):
    • - stand(shake head): you keep your points and wait until round end
    • - hit(nod): you roll another dice (one at a time) until you bust or choose to stand
    • - double down(giggle): you double your bet and take one more roll
    • - split(mumble): if dealt two cards of the same value you can put second bet and each card becomes a separate hand
    • - surrender(groan): if you don't like your initial hand you can surrender for half of your bet
    21 in initial deal = instant 3:2 of your bet Same amount as dealer = keep your bet
  • 4.After all players stand/bust Dealer hits until they bust / reach 17 or higher. Dealer announces winners/losers.
  • 5.After updating their token amount players can spend them on things in prices tab.
  • If your tokens go into negative check the backrupcy tab
  • You roll dice using /dice 10 command.

Bankruptcy

  • If your token amount is negative you are considered bankrupt
  • Bankrupt player (BP) gets jailed in cage by the dealer and can:
    1. Play and try to win their freedom
    2. Wait to be bought
  • Free players can spend money to buy caged players or interact with them as in prices tab
  • If BP decides to play they receive 40 tokens and must play until they can buy themselves or go bankrupt again. Minimum bet is reduced to 10.
  • If BP bankrupts again they are presented with the choice again. If they decide to play their value is decreased and given less tokens as below:
    • First:(initial) 40 tokens | 150 to buy | 200 to strip
    • Second: 30 tokens | 100 to buy | 150 to strip
    • Third: 20 tokens | 50 to buy | 100 to strip
  • 4th bankrupcy BP is proposed with ultimatum:
    1. Wait to be bought for the lowest price
    2. All or nothing against dealer
      • WINBP is set free
      • LOSSBP becomes dealer property
  • In case of no active players BP can be released but are required to spend 1hr in restraints chosen by dealer

Purchase

  • Between rounds players can buy stuff listed in prices tab.
  • If a player gets bought they become owned by the buyer and are required to obey to them
  • Owners can do whatever they want with their new prize, including leaving with them or setting them free
