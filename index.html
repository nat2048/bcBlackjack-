Basic rules

All players begin with 50 tokens, display and keep count of them on a sign

The minimum bet is 20 tokens

The game uses a 10-sided die (/dice 10). Numbers 2-10 are worth their face value. The number 1 acts like an Ace (1 or 11, whichever is beneficial).

The goal is to be closest to 21 without going over

>

After betting dealer rolls one die, players take two initial rolls and then decide:

  • hit - take another roll, can be repeated until reaching 21 or over
  • stand - keep current rolls, can be used after hitting or splitting
  • double down - double the bet and take one more roll then stand
  • split - only if initial rolls have same value, double your bet and create two separate hands, each gets one more initial roll and then decide again

After each player stands or busts(gets over 21) dealer takes their rolls until they reach 17 or bust.

If you don't have enough tokens to bet you can get a loan, if you win you get the amount you loaned, if you lose you bankrupt

Bankrupcy & Jail

If player bankrupts they are jailed and presented with a decision: wait or play

  • Wait - you wait to be bought by another player but they might keep you for a while
  • Play - you start again with 40 tokens, 10 minimum bet and you are forced to play every round, you need to earn tokens to free yourself

Jailed players can be bought by free players but if they decide to play their price is increased by 50 tokens

If jailed player bankrupts again they get they can play again with 10 fewer tokens but their price drops by 50

If player bankrupts 5 times they lose and their fate is up to the dealer
